What Entrepreneurs Get

Value 01

X10 AI Literacy

In two days, you may move through a learning curve that would normally take months. Not through lectures, but by working side by side with AI-native builders who search, code, prototype, and iterate with AI as a natural medium.

  • Learn by watching real AI workflows
  • Cross practical thresholds in prompting, research, coding, and prototyping
  • Understand what AI can actually do for your business
  • Leave with sharper instincts, not just notes
Value 02

A Meaningful Prototype

Bring a real challenge, workflow, opportunity, or product idea. During the sprint, you can form a team and turn it into a prototype, demo, internal tool, or first working version.

  • Prototype around your own business request
  • Test an idea quickly
  • See how young AI-native builders approach complexity
  • Leave with a concrete next step

How The Sprint Works

01

Bring a challenge

Come with a business idea, workflow, pain point, product hypothesis, or innovation question.

02

Meet the talent core

Entrepreneurs and young builders meet, discuss possible directions, and choose who they want to work with.

03

Build in public

Teams work in the same physical space, using AI, code, research, and fast prototyping.

04

Present to judges

Final projects are presented and the winning team receives the prize.
